Top 5 Hills of Rajasthan

1

Mount Abu

A picturesque place with magnificent architectural structures and luxuriant greenery, one of the small yet glorious hill stations of Rajasthan, Mount Abu looks absolutely awe-striking during the daytime. Due to its gorgeous scenery and scenic backdrop of Aravalli Mountain, this pretty hill station receives thousands of visitors every year from all over the world. Besides the enchanting scenery, these famous hill stations in Rajasthan for couples also offer numerous temples to its travelers, which are known for their architectural brilliance. Amidst these holy places, the Dilwara Temples are quite popular among the tourists because of the intricate carvings and ornamented interiors.

  • Travel, Lifestyle
  • 163km away from the city of Udaipur

2

Guru Shikhar

Affectionately known as the Peak of the Guru, the Guru Shikhar Hill Station is widely considered by the tourists as a land of tranquility. Acknowledged as one of the most famous hill stations of Rajasthan, it was after the god, Dattatreya, who was staying on this place during his early days. Located quite close to the Mount Abu hill stations in Rajasthan, Guru Shikhar hill station offers excellent panoramic views to the visitors. Along with enjoying the beautiful scenery, you can also indulge in numerous adventurous activities, such as trekking, here. Because of being located far away from the bustling city life, the environment of this place also stays quite calm and serene.

  • Travel, Lifestyle
  • 10.5km away from Mount Abu

3

Sajjangarh

Located atop a serene hill, the majestic palace of Sajjangarh looks quite enthralling with a mesmerizing mountain range as a backdrop. Established in the year of 1884, this beautiful place is considered to be an ideal summer haven for tourists, who are trying to stay away from the heat of summer. Branded as one of the top hill stations in Rajasthan by the tourists, the beautiful palace offers an exquisite panoramic view of the Udaipur city. From this palace, you will also be able to experience the breathtaking view of the Fateh Sagar Lake will stay in your memory for days. It is one of the beautiful hill stations of Rajasthan.

  • Travel, Lifestyle
  • 11.4km away from Udaipur

4

Achalgarh

Carefully nestled in the beautiful Aravalli range, Achalgarh looks like a subtle piece of paradise when viewed from the ground. Known as one of the best hill stations in Rajasthan, it is located only 11 km away from Mount Abu, which makes it an ideal weekend getaway from the nearby towns like Jodhpur and Udaipur. Most people generally travel to Achalgarh to experience the serenity of the mountains. However, if you have an adventurous mind, then you can also indulge in trekking here.

  • Travel, Lifestyle
  • 173km from Udaipur

5

Ranakpur

Nestled in the elongated Aravalli Mountain range, Ranakpur is one of the small village hill stations of Rajasthan, which is known for its eloquent natural beauty. Besides these, this place also houses numerous historically important architectures, which are quite famous among people. Due to these reasons, most tourists consider this place to be one of the best places for a weekend getaway from the metropolises like Jodhpur, Udaipur, and Jaipur. Known as one of the best hill stations in Rajasthan, Ranakpur will transfix you with its ethereal landscapes and its beautiful Jain temples. So, if you are a nature-aficionado, then you will surely fall in love with this tiny piece of paradise.

  • Travel, Lifestyle
  • 162 km away from Jodhpur
